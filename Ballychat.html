How to turn off auto-renew and cancel channel subscriptions on your Roku streaming device:

Google Play

  1. Open the Google Play app.

  4. Select the subscription or prepaid plan you want to cancel.

Account Management

Pause Guide

    To temporarily pause your Bally Sports+ subscription, please follow these steps:
    1. Visit https://www.ballysports.com/my-account
    2. Click on the "Subscriptions" tab.
    3. Locate the "Active Subscriptions" section and find the "Pause Your Subscription" option.
    4. Next to "Pause Your Subscription," click on the "Pause" button.
    5. A menu will appear, allowing you to select the duration of the pause. You can choose from 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, or 6 months.
    6. Once you have decided on the desired pause duration, click the "Pause" button to confirm your selection.
By following these steps, you will successfully pause your Bally Sports+ subscription for the chosen duration.

_____________

Note:

During the pause period, the subscription is inactive, and no charges are incurred. However, once the pause period expires, the subscription automatically resumes, ensuring uninterrupted service.

Upon resumption, the billing date is adjusted based on the number of days remaining from the previous subscription period. This means that the paused period's remaining days will be added to the new subscription, allowing users to fully utilize the subscription period they paid for.

Update CC Details: Web

To update your credit card details on your Bally Sports account, please follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your Bally Sports account using your email address and password.
  2. Once logged in, click on the "My Account" button in the screen's top-right corner.
  3. Under "My Account," select the "Subscriptions" tab.
  4. On the "Billing Details," you will see your current credit card information. To update your credit card details, click on the "Edit" button.
  5. Enter your new credit card information, including the card number, expiration date, and security code.
  6. Click on the "Save" button to update your credit card details.

A confirmation message will appear to confirm that your credit card information has been updated successfully. Please note that changes to your credit card information may take up to 24 hours to take effect on your account.

Couch Rights

Couch Rights is a Bally Sports+ subscription feature that allows you to watch premium content on your devices outside of your home billing zip code for up to 30 days. It will be activated automatically once you begin watching premium content from a location that is more than 50 miles away from the center of your home location or the zip code registered on the account. And if you lose access to premium content because your couch rights expire after 30 days, you must log in to the Bally Sports app with location services enabled within a 50-mile radius of the center of your account's registered home zip code to restore services.
